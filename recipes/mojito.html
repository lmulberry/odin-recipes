Mojito

A Classic Cuban Cocktail, the Mojito

This classis drink is sure to wow your guests, as it tastes just as good as it looks!

Simple to make and refreshing, it's a perfect goto drink for summer get togethers.

Ingredients:

  • 2oz white rum
  • Lime juice (approx. 3/4oz), freshly squeezed from half a lime
  • 2 to 3 lime wedges
  • 3/4oz simple syrup
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Club soda, to top off
  • Ice (cubed or crushed)
  • Lime wedge/mint sprig for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place 4-5 mint leaves and 2-3 lime wedges at the bottom of an empty glass
  2. Gently muddle the mint leaves and lime wedges together
  3. Fill glass with ice
  4. Add white rum
  5. Add lime juice
  6. Add simple syrup
  7. Gently stir
  8. Top off with club soda
  9. Garnish with lime wedge and/or mint sprig if you'd like and enjoy!
